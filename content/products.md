### PetiteKnit — important fulfilment rule
We **cannot sell digital downloads** of PetiteKnit patterns — the designer directs digital sales to Ravelry. **Exception:** for **kits**, we may buy the digital pattern from Ravelry at B2B cost, print it in store, and include the printed pattern in a yarn + pattern bundle. This means **PetiteKnit kit pages can always be fulfilled**, even when the standalone printed pattern is out of stock.

## Kits
Curated yarn + pattern bundles, broadly in three types:
- **Orry Yarn kits** — our yarn paired with our patterns.
- **PetiteKnit kits** — yarn paired with an in-house-printed PetiteKnit pattern; always fulfillable per the rule above.
- **Branded sealed kits** — pre-packaged kits.
