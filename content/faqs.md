## Orry Yarn
**What is Orry Yarn?**
Orry Yarn is our own British-made yarn — 100% Merino, DK weight (50g / ~115 m per ball), spun and dyed in Yorkshire, in a 16-colour palette inspired by the Scottish landscape.

## Patterns
**Do you sell PetiteKnit patterns?**
We stock **printed** PetiteKnit patterns. We don't sell PetiteKnit **digital** downloads — those are available directly via Ravelry.

**The printed pattern I want is sold out — what can I do?**
If the pattern is available as a **kit**, we can include a printed copy with the yarn, so the kit can usually be fulfilled even when the standalone printed pattern is out of stock.
